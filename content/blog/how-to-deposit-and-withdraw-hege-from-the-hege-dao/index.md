Learn how to deposit and withdraw $HEGE from the Hege DAO.

<!--more-->

*This post is part of a series of posts about the Hege DAO, see [How To Use the
Hege DAO](/blog/how-to-use-the-hege-dao/) for an introduction and overview.*

## How to deposit and withdraw 

**NOTE: If you have never deposited into the Hege DAO before, please follow the
instructions in [How to Become a Member of the Hege
DAO](/blog/how-to-become-a-member-of-the-hege-dao/).**

If you have made the initial deposit into the Hege DAO, follow the instructions
below to deposit more $HEGE, or to withdraw some of your deposited tokens.

## How to deposit $HEGE into the Hege DAO

1. Go to the [Hege DAO page on Realms](https://v2.realms.today/dao/Farke9p1ZDCwDp94rWGut2jaWHogVcGc1TRU6Vd7SeTy). It should look something like this. Click "Connect Wallet" in the upper right corner of the page to connect your wallet.

{{< figure
src="v2-connect-1.png"
>}}

2. If necessary, select your Solana wallet to complete the connection.

{{< figure
src="v2-connect-2.png"
>}}

3. When you have connected your wallet, you can see how many $HEGE tokens you have deposited in the upper right corner, right next to the banner.

{{< figure
src="v2-deposit-0.png"
>}}

4. Click "Deposit".

{{< figure
src="v2-deposit-1.png"
>}}

5. Select how many $HEGE you want to deposit.

{{< figure
src="v2-deposit-2.png"
>}}

6. Then click "Deposit".

{{< figure
src="v2-deposit-3.png"
>}}

7. Confirm the transaction in your Solana wallet.

{{< figure
src="v2-deposit-4.png"
>}}

8. That's it! When the transaction has confirmed on the Solana blockchain, the page will update to show your new balance.

{{< figure
src="v2-deposit-5.png"
>}}

## How to withdraw $HEGE from the Hege DAO 

1. Go to the [Hege DAO page on
Realms](https://v2.realms.today/dao/Farke9p1ZDCwDp94rWGut2jaWHogVcGc1TRU6Vd7SeTy).
It should look something like this. Click "Connect Wallet" in the upper right
corner of the page to connect your wallet.

{{< figure
src="v2-connect-1.png"
>}}

2. If necessary, select your Solana wallet to complete the connection.

{{< figure
src="v2-connect-2.png"
>}}

3. When you have connected your wallet, you can see how many $HEGE tokens you have deposited in the upper right corner, right next to the banner.

{{< figure
src="v2-connect-3.png"
>}}

4. Click "Withdraw".

{{< figure
src="v2-withdraw-1.png"
>}}

5. Select how many $HEGE you want to withdraw.

{{< figure
src="v2-withdraw-2.png"
>}}

6. Then click "Withdraw".

{{< figure
src="v2-withdraw-3.png"
>}}

7. Confirm the transaction in your Solana wallet.

{{< figure
src="v2-withdraw-4.png"
>}}

8. That's it! When the transaction has confirmed on the Solana blockchain, the page will update to show your new balance.

{{< figure
src="v2-withdraw-5.png"
>}}
