Learn how to create a spending request for unlocking funds in the Hege DAO.

<!--more-->

*This post is part of a series of posts about the Hege DAO, see [How To Use the
Hege DAO](/blog/how-to-use-the-hege-dao/) for an introduction and overview.*

## How to create a spending request

**NOTE: To create a spending request, you must first be a member of the Hege
DAO. If you are not already a member, please follow the instructions in [How to
Become a Member of the Hege DAO](/blog/how-to-become-a-member-of-the-hege-dao/),
and then come back here.**

This article will show how to create a **spending proposal** for spending funds
from the DAO treasury. (If you want to make a poll proposal to just get the
opinion about something from the members in the Hege DAO, see [How to Create a
Poll in the Hege DAO](/blog/how-to-create-a-poll-in-the-hege-dao/) instead.

## When you are a member of the Hege DAO, here is how to create a spending request

 1. Go to the [Hege DAO page on
 Realms](https://v2.realms.today/dao/Farke9p1ZDCwDp94rWGut2jaWHogVcGc1TRU6Vd7SeTy).
 Connect your wallet. It should look something like this. You can see how many
 $HEGE tokens you have deposited in the upper right corner, right next to the
 banner. *(NOTE: You must have at least 10,000 $HEGE deposited to make a
 proposal. See [How to Deposit and
 Withdraw](/blog/how-to-deposit-and-withdraw-hege-from-the-hege-dao/) to learn
 how to deposit tokens.)*

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-1-1.png"
>}}

2. Click "Create Proposal". 

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-1-2.png"
>}}

3. Fill in the title and description of your proposal.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-2-1.png"
>}}

4. Select the Vote Type to "Executable". Then click the "Next" button.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-2-2.png"
>}}

5. Click "Add Instruction".

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-3-1.png"
>}}

6. Click "Send Tokens".

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-3-2.png"
>}}

7. Select what token to send, the receiving Solana address, and the amount.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-3-3.png"
>}}

8. Click "Next".

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-3-4.png"
>}}

9. Review the information that you filled in. Make sure everything is correct.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-4-1.png"
>}}

10. Select "No" on vote by multi-sig. Then click "Create Proposal".

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-4-2.png"
>}}

9. Confirm the transaction in your Solana wallet.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-5.png"
>}}

10. Your proposal has now been created! If a share dialog is shown, close it.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-6.png"
>}}

11. You now see a page showing information about your proposal. Go back to the main page of the Hege DAO by clicking on "Hege DAO" in the upper left corner of the page.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-7.png"
>}}

12. Back at the main page, you can now see your new proposal in the list of proposals.

{{< figure
src="v2-create-spend-request-8.png"
>}}

13. That's it! Your proposal is created and open for voting. Now go share it
with the Hege community! And don't forget to vote on your proposal. See [How to
Vote in the Hege DAO](/blog/how-to-vote-in-the-hege-dao/) if you need help with that.
